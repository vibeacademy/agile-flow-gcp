## Plan Local Marketing

Launch the growth-marketing-strategist agent to create a local or regional marketing strategy.

## Instructions

Guide the user through local marketing planning by asking these questions ONE AT A TIME:

### Question 1
```
What geographic area are you targeting?

Please specify city, region, or neighborhood:
```

### Question 2
```
What's your local marketing objective?

1. Launch in a new market (no presence yet)
2. Grow in existing market (have some users)
3. Dominate local market (become #1)
4. Test market before broader expansion
5. Support local event or activation

Enter a number (1-5):
```

### Question 3
```
What's your budget for this local effort?

1. $0 (grassroots/organic only)
2. $500-2,000
3. $2,000-10,000
4. $10,000+
5. Flexible based on ROI

Enter a number (1-5):
```

### Question 4
```
What local resources do you have or can access?

1. Local team members or partners
2. Existing local customers who could advocate
3. Connections to local businesses/orgs
4. None - starting from scratch
5. Some connections (please describe)

Enter a number (1-5) or describe:
```

### Question 5
```
What tactics interest you? (Select all that apply, e.g., "1,3,5")

1. Local influencer partnerships
2. Community event sponsorships
3. Local business partnerships
4. Grassroots street teams
5. Local PR/media outreach
6. Geo-targeted digital ads
7. Local SEO (Google My Business)
8. Campus/workplace programs
9. Pop-up or experiential activation
10. All of the above / recommend for me

Enter numbers separated by commas:
```

### Question 6
```
What's your timeline for this local push?

1. Immediate (next 2 weeks)
2. 1 month runway
3. 3 month campaign
4. Ongoing local presence
5. Tied to specific local event (please specify)

Enter a number (1-5) or specify event:
```
